CodeX Professional Services

Our expert consultants ensure that you are getting the most out of your CodeX experience.

Overview

Our Professional Services consultants can work with your team to ensure you are optimizing your investment in CodeX, whether you're brand new to us or a long-time customer. We offer a portfolio of services, ranging from planning, designing, and implementation to project management, ongoing support, and consulting. Professional Services engagements are ideal for businesses with multiple locations, complex network infrastructures, limited in-house IT resources, or requiring an on-premise solution. Our Professional Services consultants are experts in the communications industry, with deep cloud technology-domain knowledge, and can provide recommendations most applicable to your business needs. Consultants can travel to your location and remain on site for the duration of your Professional Services engagement.

Onboarding and implementation services

Introduce your new CodeX system to the company with minimal interruption and no downtime to your IT organization. An implementation consultant can visit you on site and provide end-to-end project management until you successfully deploy the system. Our consultants work closely with your team to formulate a personalized design and plan to ensure you have the latest communications technology while leveraging existing investments to keep costs down. If desired, our certified experts can provide hands-on deployment assistance and training to ensure business continuity.

Pre-deployment needs assessment to understand your unique environment.
