Efficient communications for a better customer experience.

CodeX Call Center is cloud based, helping customers connect with the right agent and eliminating the frustrations of customer support.

Keep your customers happy and loyal with an enhanced call center experience

CodeX Call Center is an omnichannel solution that lets customers choose their preferred method of communicating with your company, including voice, chat, social media, SMS, email, and more. Intelligent IVR and self-service options--tightly integrated with smart routing functionality--help customers connect more quickly to the agent who can best handle their needs. CodeX produces real-time reports and analytics, helping you optimize staff scheduling, improve call-center efficiencies, survey customers, and more. Plus, CodeX works seamlessly with Salesforce and other popular CRM platforms.
